About My Medi Buddy

I made My Medi Buddy to make managing your health a little less stressful.

It's a simple Android app where you can track medications, store important info, and keep notes, all in one place.

I wanted something that feels easy, calm, and genuinely helpful day to day.

My Medi Buddy is all about giving you more control and peace of mind, without the overwhelm.
