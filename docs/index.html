About My Medi Buddy

I made My Medi Buddy to take some of the stress out of managing your health.

It's a simple Android app where you can keep everything in one place, track your medications, store important medical info, and jot down notes about how you're feeling or anything you don't want to forget.

I know how overwhelming it can be trying to keep on top of everything, especially when it comes to your health. So I wanted to build something that feels easy to use, calm, and actually helpful day to day.

My Medi Buddy is all about giving you a bit more control and peace of mind, without making things complicated.
