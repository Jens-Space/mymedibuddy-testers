Send Feedback 💬

Found something? We'd love to hear from you. Here's how to reach us.

Use the In-App Form

The best way to report bugs is directly through the app — it automatically includes helpful technical details.

  1. Open the My Medi Buddy app
  2. Tap the Menu (three lines or profile icon)
  3. Select Send Feedback
  4. Fill out the form and tap Submit

What to Include

  • What happened? Describe the issue in as much detail as you can.
  • Steps to reproduce — What were you doing right before it happened?
  • What you expected — What should have happened instead?
  • Screenshot — Really helpful if you can add one!

Why Use the In-App Form?

When you submit through the app, we automatically get:

  • Your device model and Android version
  • App version number
  • Recent app activity

This helps us diagnose and fix issues much faster! 🚀

Priority Levels

Here's how we prioritize issues:

  • Critical — App crashes, data loss, safety issues
  • High — Major features broken, frequent crashes
  • Medium — Minor bugs, UI issues
  • Low — Suggestions, minor improvements
